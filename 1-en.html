A sentence written by oneself or quoted from the existing article becomes the starting point of the artwork. As sentences made by individual authors are gathered together, it composes a paragraph.

However, that paragraph does not contextually make sense, since sentences are gathered from different sources. Paradoxically, we pay attention to each individual sentence because of the nonsensical content of the paragraph. Therefore, we chose paragraph as title of the exhibition. So the title covers all the sentences of each author without any changes or distortions.

Shall we take a look at the artwork one by one with ‘a sentence’? The exhibition that unified visual identity will soon be held.
